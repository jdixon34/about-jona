About Jona

Hi my name is Jonathan, but I go by Jona. It's a stage name for my music career that I am still trying to get off the ground. I've been a singer-songwriter/bedroom producer for about 7 years now. I've been playing guitar for about 10 years now, and I only learned because of a girl. After falling in love with guitar I became obsessed with music.

Than in 2019 I released a debut album titled, "Simple Mind". I recorded, performed, and produced the entire album by myself, with the exception of using Garage band for their drum samples and drum machine. I then spent about a year or two in the Albuquerque music scene and had the surprise of my life. The birth of my baby boy, "Kaladin", is what drives me today. That is the whole reason why I'm in this bootcamp because I know I can do a web developers job.

Also, I am an ultra-marathon runner and I used to compete in the Ironman races. I can run at crazy speed because I train high up there in the Sandia mountains. Whenever I come across a wild mountain lion, I just speed-climb up a tree! It's quite SENSATIONAL!
